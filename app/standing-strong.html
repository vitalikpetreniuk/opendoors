Standing Strong 2022

You are warmly invited to join Open Doors supporters for our annual gathering, together again in-person, at this year’s Standing Strong on Saturday 24 September 2022 at Trent Vineyard, Nottingham.

Evening event 7:30-9pm

Attend the evening event, in-person or watch online. Join us and meet your persecuted church family from the Middle East and Asia. You will be encouraged by their stories of courage and faith and have an opportunity to unite with them in prayer and worship.

Attend In-person

Attend Online

Afternoon seminars 3-5pm

This year, in-person attendees also have the opportunity to attend two afternoon seminars. There will be a choice of three seminars: ‘Living in Afghanistan the most dangerous place to be a Christian’, ‘How hope is being restored in the Middle East’ and ‘The global trends of persecution.’

Resorts World Arena update

As we are still emerging from Covid, we believe it is not appropriate, at this time, to host a large-scale in-person event. We have therefore decided to postpone our booking at Resorts World Arena to 2023.

If you have ticket(s) booked for the Standing Strong event taking place at Resorts World Arena, all of your tickets - including any youth, children, family, group and lunch tickets that you may have ordered - will now be automatically transferred to 2023.

Please note, full refunds are available. To request a refund, please do this via the account of your ticket provider. If you booked directly through Ticket Factory, please contact them and request a refund through this form. You can find more details within our FAQs below.

Those who booked over the phone with our Inspire team will have booked via Eventbrite. Details on how to request a refund via your Eventbrite account can be found here. You can find more details within our FAQs below.

If you have booked tickets on behalf of others, please notify them also as we don’t hold their contact details.
